Is MyCoinPoll audited or secure?

Yes, our smart contracts have been independently audited and verified. You can view the full audit report and contract address on Etherscan.

Is MyCoinPoll legally registered?

Yes. MyCoinPoll is operated by MetaFusion Labs, a legally registered company in Panama authorized to raise public funds through token sales.

What is the ECM Token used for?

The ECM token enables staking, voting, discounted trading, governance, and future access to metaverse features and NFT utilities within the ecosystem.

How is the ECM Token distributed?

ECM has a total supply of 200 million tokens. 24 percent is allocated for public sale. The rest is distributed among staking rewards, ecosystem development, team, and partners.

Will ECM be listed on exchanges?

Yes. ECM is scheduled to be listed on bCoinMart at the end of Q1 2026, with other exchange listings planned after that.

When will I be able to vote on new projects?

The voting feature will go live after the ECM token is listed on bCoinMart and the launchpad opens to external projects.

Can I participate in other projects before the ICO ends?

No. Currently, the platform is focused on hosting the ECM ICO. Additional projects will be launched after the ECM listing.

Is KYC required to use MyCoinPoll?

At this stage, KYC is not required to participate in the ECM ICO. However, future token sales may require KYC based on regulatory needs.

Where is user data stored?

User data is securely encrypted and stored on protected servers following industry-standard data protection and privacy measures.
